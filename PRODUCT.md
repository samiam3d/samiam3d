# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creative directors, game studios, collaborators, and recruiters reviewing Sam's art direction, interactive design, and world-building work across major entertainment brands and original projects.

## Product Purpose

Present Sam's portfolio as a confident, image-led body of work. The site should make the breadth and quality of the work immediately clear, invite exploration, and make direct contact effortless.

## Brand Personality

Bold, playful, kinetic. The experience should feel authored by a game art director: visual first, technically sharp, and alive without competing with the work.

## Anti-references

Generic portfolio templates, corporate agency language, repetitive hero copy, static image dumps, tiny editorial typography, and decorative effects that make the artwork harder to see.

## Design Principles

- Let the work carry the page.
- Use typography as a recognizable signature.
- Make interaction feel playful and deliberate.
- Keep navigation quiet until it is useful.
- Preserve clarity and speed beneath every visual effect.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard navigation. Respect reduced-motion preferences, retain visible focus states, provide descriptive image text, and keep every modal operable without a pointer.
